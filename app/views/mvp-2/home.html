Find local family support

Using this service you can find your nearest family hub as well as other support services in the local area.

Family hubs

Family hubs are welcoming places to go that bring together a wide range of local support, groups and services in one place for the whole family. If you’re looking for general family services and activities near you, family hubs are a good place to start. You can drop in, meet other families and find out what’s available in your area.

Finding help with something

If you’re looking for a specific type of support or group, you can also search here for all kinds of family services that run across different family hubs and other clinics and community centres near you. Examples of services and groups you can search for include:

  • help with child development
  • groups and classes for babies, toddlers, young people, parents and carers
  • support for parents and carers, such as with infant feeding
  • children’s health clinics
  • classes and workshops
  • support for special educational needs and disabilities (SEND)
  • mental health support
  • help with relationships

Family hubs, services and groups are available for:

  • babies
  • children
  • young people up to age 19 (up to 25 with SEND)
  • parents and carers

As this service is new, not all local authority services will be listed.
