Find local family support

Find your nearest family hub, groups and services

Using this service, you can find your nearest family hub. Family hubs are welcoming places where you can drop in and find a wide range of local support, groups and services in one place for the whole family. If you’re looking for general family services and activities near you, family hubs are a good place to start.

If you’re looking for a specific type of support or activity, you can also search here for all kinds of family services run by different family hubs and organisations near you.

Who can use this service

This service is available across England for:

  • babies
  • children
  • young people up to age 19 (up to 25 with SEND)
  • parents and carers
