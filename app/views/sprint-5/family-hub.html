What is a family hub?

Family hubs bring together local services in one place for families.

Family hubs offer a wide range of services for the whole family, including:

  • babies
  • children
  • teenagers
  • parents and carers
  • children and young people with special educational needs and disabilities up to age 25

What do family hubs offer?

Each hub or children’s centre offers a different range of services and activities, for example:

  • breastfeeding support
  • child development clinics
  • parenting workshops
  • mental health help
  • groups and clubs

Where are family hubs?

There is normally a base for a family hub or children’s centre. Some services may run from different locations, like libraries or health clinics.

There are family hubs all over England, run by local councils. You can search here to find family hubs near you, or children’s centres, which may offer similar services.
