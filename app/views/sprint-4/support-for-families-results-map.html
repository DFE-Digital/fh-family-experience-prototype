Get support from Devon family hubs

Welcome to Devon family hubs. We have 21 family hubs and children’s centres spread all across the county, and also offer some services online. Our services work together to promote the health, development and well-being of all children, young people and families in Devon.

We are here to help you, whether you’re expecting a baby, or you’re the parent or carer of a child or young person up to age 19 (or 25 if they have special educational needs and disabilities).
